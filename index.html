About

Forward is an application designed to forward Yahoo! email to another email address. Yahoo! email filters do not have a fowarding option, only a move-to-folder option. So this application will loop over all the messages in your inbox, find messages matching your rules, forward the messages matching a rule to the email address configured for that rule, then delete the message from your inbox.
